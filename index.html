About

he Artificial Intelligence–Natural Language Processing–Machine Learning (AI-NLP-ML) Group in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Patna officially began its journey in June 2015. Under the leadership of Prof. Pushpak Bhattacharyya, and supported by faculty members Dr. Asif Ekbal and Dr. Sriparna Saha, the group is committed to advancing research in AI, ML, and NLP. It comprises around 45 members, including research scholars, engineers, lexicographers, and B.Tech/M.Tech students, and is currently engaged in several industry-sponsored projects.

In collaboration with the group, Elsevier has established the Elsevier Centre of Excellence for Natural Language Processing to drive innovation in emerging areas of AI, NLP, and ML. Additionally, ezDI has launched the Sushrut-eZDI Research Lab on Health Informatics, aimed at developing impactful healthcare solutions and fostering research in the domain.
